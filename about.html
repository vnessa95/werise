WHO WE ARE

We Rise is a movement to stop climate change and create millions of good jobs in the process

We're building an army of young people to make climate change an urgent priority across America, end the corrupting influence of fossil fuel executives on our politics, and elect a strong band of diverse leaders who stand up for the health and wellbeing of all people.
We are ordinary young people who are scared about what the climate crisis means for the people and places we love. We are gathering in classrooms, living rooms, and worship halls across the country. Everyone has a role to play. Public opinion is already with us - if we unite by the millions we can turn this into political power and reclaim our democracy.

We are not looking to the right or left. We look forward. Together, and we will rise to the momeent so that we can change this country and this world.

OUR STRATEGY

  • STAGE 1, 2018: Launch the movement.
  • STAGE 2, 2019: Make climate change a priority in the 2020 presidential election.
  • STAGE 3, 2020: Make the entire country feel the ergency of the crisis.
  • STAGE 4, 2020: Win governing power by bringing it home through the 2020 general election.
  • STAGE 4, 2021: Engage in mass noncooperation to interup business as usual and win a Green New Deal

OUR THEORY OF CHANGE

  • PEOPLE POWER: An active base of public support
  • We build our people power by talking to people. We also grow our people power through escalated moral protest.
  • POLITICAL POWER: A critical mass of enthusiastically supportive public officals.
  • We vote out corrupt officials and vote in real leaders who will stand with us.
  • THE PEOPLES ALIGNMENT: Movement and groups united by the shared vision of a goverment that fights for dignity and justice for all.
